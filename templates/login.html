About This App

The Summer Camp Organization Tool helps families:

  • Track camp ideas and preferences for each child
  • Manage registration deadlines and bookings
  • Integrate with Google Calendar for reminders
  • Coordinate schedules across multiple children
  • Keep track of camp details and costs

Note: This app is private. Only authorized email addresses can access it.
